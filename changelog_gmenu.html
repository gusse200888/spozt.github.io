How to Install:
1. Install ScriptHookV and ScriptHookVDotNet. You can find how to do that on YouTube.
2. Create a folder called: "scripts" (Without the quotes of course) in your GTA V Main Directory.
3. Download Gmenu.dll in this release and drag it into the scripts folder.
4. Download NativeUI and drag it into the scripts folder.
5. Enjoy.
ScriptHookV: https://www.gta5-mods.com/tools/script-hook-v
ScriptHookVDotNet: https://www.gta5-mods.com/tools/scripthookv-net
NativeUI: https://gtaforums.com/topic/809284-net-nativeui/
